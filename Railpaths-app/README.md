Bangladesh Railway does not offer a free, open-source live map tracking API, passengers are often forced to wait blindly on crowded platforms due to unannounced schedule shifts. Arohi Rail bridges this exact gap. By connecting an administrative ticketing core directly with a decentralized, station-master-driven checkpoint tracking network, the platform translates real-time platform departures into dynamic, forward-propagated ETA updates. This allows passengers to stay safely at home or work until their delayed train is actually approaching their station.
